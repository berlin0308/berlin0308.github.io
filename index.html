Po-Lin (Berlin) Chen

Po-Lin Chen from Taipei, Taiwan


I recently graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Biomechatronics Engineering from National Taiwan University (NTU). Currently, I am a research assistant @ Biophotonics and Bioimaging Lab (BBLab), under the guidance of Prof. Ta-Te Lin.

My research and career interests include computer vision, robotics, and internet of things (IoT). I aim to drive meaningful progress in various fields with the innovative use of these technologies.

Previously, I interned as a software engineer @ Advantech's Edge Vision AI team, where I proposed and optimized a multimodal anomaly detection system in retail using Intel’s OpenVINO C++ Toolkit.

I'm ready to pursue my M.S./M.Eng. in CS/EE/CV, focusing on visual computing, intelligent systems, and AI.
