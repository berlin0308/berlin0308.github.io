I like building things. Some are practical, some are just for fun, and some are questionable ideas that somehow worked out.

Whether it is computer vision, robotics, IoT, or some wild mix of the three, I love exploring how machines can perceive the world, making tiny devices into unexpectedly powerful, and watching AI stumble (or thrive) in everyday scenarios.

Smart agriculture, intelligent retail, and education are just a few playgrounds where I turn ideas into reality -- sometimes they solve real-world problems, other times they just make life a little more interesting. Either way, I’m always up for the next challenge.
