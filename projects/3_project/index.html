GREENIFY: Your Recycle Mentor - An Autonomous Recycling Bin Born for Environmental Education & Social Good

#Computer-Vision #Embedded-ML #Sustainability

Poster | Presentation | Demo

Credit: Me, Google Taiwan, 2023 Google Hardware Product Sprint



Our goal was to promote environmental sustainability in schools for young students, making recycling fun and interactive.

During the 2023 Google Hardware Sprint, I worked as a backend engineer, focusing on efficient neural network inference on embedded hardware for waste classification, the core technology of our project. I also handled software-hardware integration and backend-frontend communication to ensure our AI components operate smoothly with the physical recycling bin.

We built an embedded intelligent educational recycling bin that combined AI-driven waste sorting with immersive storylines, where users' choices shaped different outcomes. Working closely with hardware engineers, front-end developers, UI/UX designers, and industrial designers, we captured students' attention and effectively encouraged better habits in a playful, impactful way.

The project was awarded as "Best Product of the Year" as well as "Product for Social Good" by Google Taiwan.
